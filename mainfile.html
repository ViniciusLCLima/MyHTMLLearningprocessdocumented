Headings

Headings are made by putting an H letter at the start of a tag, putting the correspont number of the type of heading you want and then putting the heading content and then closing it with its closing tag.

This an H1, it is the greater of the h# elements

This is an H2, it is smaller than the h1. According to the book, can be used as a sub topic of the h1

This is an h3, desined to be a sub topic of the h2, and so on with h4, h5 and 6
